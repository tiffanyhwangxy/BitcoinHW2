About This Indicator

The Premium to NAV (Net Asset Value) calculates the market valuation of MicroStrategy (MSTR) relative to the underlying value of the Bitcoin it holds. A positive premium indicates the market is valuing MSTR's equity higher than its strict Bitcoin treasury value, often reflecting strong institutional demand for regulated BTC exposure.
